> so who is twenty one pilots?

twenty one pilots is an alternative band formed in 2009 by tyler joseph (vocals and instrumentals), nick thomas (bass), and chris salih (drums). in 2011, josh dun would replace thomas and salih as the present drummer. twenty one pilots have 6 official albums, 1 unofficial album, 2 live albums, 1 EP, and 10 singles in their discography.

> about tyler joseph

tyler joseph was born december 1st, 1988 in columbus, ohio. he is married to his spouse jenna joseph, who he has 3 kids with, rosie, junie, and tommy. before forming twenty one pilots, he recorded an independent album, no phun intended, released in 2007.

> about josh dun

josh dun was born june 18th, 1988 in columbus, ohio. he is married to his spouse debby ryan, they are parents to his dog jim. he was briefly in the band house of heroes for seven months of 2010, filling in for the drummer. josh was introduced to tyler through chris salih at his job at guitar center.
