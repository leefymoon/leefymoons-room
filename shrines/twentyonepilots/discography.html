> heathens (2016)

heathens was released on june 16th, 2016 as a song for the movie suicide squad (2016). the song was released along with a music vidoe a few months before the movie released.

> cancer (2016)

cancer was released on september 14th, 2016 as one of the covers for rock sound magazine's "the black parade" cover album. there was a lyric video uploaded to youtube a day before the song released officially.

> topxmm (2016)

  1. heathens

  3. ride ★

> location sessions (2019)

the location sessions were singles released throughout the second half of 2019. they were alternate versions of songs off trench recorded in different locations. the first location session "chlorine (mexico city)" released on june 20th, 2019. , the second location session "cut my lip (brooklyn)" released on july 11th, 2019. the last location session "the Hype (berlin)" released on october 16th, 2019.

> level of concern (2020)

level of concern was released on april 9th, 2020. a 'live from outside' version was later released on streaming services, debuting on jimmy fallon.

> christmas saves the year (2020)

christmas saves the year was released on december 8th, 2020 as a holiday song. this single is my favorite because at the time of release my sister lived far away and only came home for christmas so it's a very sentimental song for me.

> doubt demo (2025)

the doubt demo was released on april 9th, 2025 after it was debuted during the poland show of the clancy tour. it was released because the demo was going viral on tiktok.
